    • Adform Cookies

    Adform uses cookies for different purposes. When we refer to the term "cookies", we mean cookies and other identifiers, such as mobile advertising identifiers. Generally, cookies enable our clients to target ads to users, to evaluate how well their ads work, and to see users’ opt-out choices. For detailed information on our cookies usage purposes and for general commitment towards our platform and services privacy, see at https://site.adform.com/privacy-center/platform-privacy/product-and-services-privacy-policy/.

     

    The following table below describes cookies set by Adform on visitor’s browser:

    Name

    Description

    Expiration Period

    C

    Identifies if user’s browser accepts cookies.
    1 – Cookies are allowed
    3 – Opt-out

    If C=1 - 60 days
    If C=3 - 3650 days

    TPC

    Identifies if user’s browser accepts third party cookies

    14 days

    GCM

    Identifies if there is a need to re-check partner‘s cookie matching existence

    1 day

    CM

    Identifies if there is a need to re-check partner‘s cookie matching existence (set by AdServing)

    1 day

    CM14

    Identifies if there is a need to re-check partner‘s cookie matching existence (set by Cookie Matching)

    14 days

    token

    Security token for opt out functionality

    session

    otsid

    Advertiser specific opt-out

    3650 days

    uid

    Unique identifier

    60 days

    SR<RotatorID>

    Sequential rotator information – contains total impressions, daily impressions, total clicks, daily clicks, and last impression date

    1 day

    Identifies last click membership for 3rd party pixels on advertiser’s pages

    1 hour
