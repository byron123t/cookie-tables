Our Cookie Policy

Last Updated: May 31, 2019

Crownpeak Technology, Inc. ("us", "we", or "our") and our service providers use cookies and other similar tracking technologies on the https://www.crownpeak.com website to collect information that helps us improve the quality of our site and services and the online experience of our visitors and users.

What is a Cookie ?

A cookie is a small piece of data (text file) sent to your browser by a website you visit. A cookie file is stored on your device based on a request from your browser and allows us or a third party to remember information about you in order to enhance your experience on our website.

Why Does Crownpeak Use Cookies

Cookies are very useful and perform different tasks. First party cookies are set by us to keep track of your activity as you move through our website and enable you to move between our web pages efficiently, remember your preferences when you visit us again and generally improve your experience when browsing with us. Third party cookies are from a domain different from our website and may be used for analytics, promotional and marketing efforts to ensure that advertisements you see online are more relevant to you and your interests. We also use other tracking technologies, such as web beacons, pixels, JavaScript, and web server logs to gain analytics insights.

Cookies last for different lengths of time depending on the purpose:

  • Session cookies only last for your online session and disappear from your device when you close your browser
  • Persistent cookies stay on your device after the browser has been closed and last for the period of time specified in the cookie

Your Choices

You may opt-out of each cookie category, except for Strictly Necessary Cookies by clicking on the Cookie Consent Manager link.

If you would like to delete cookies or instruct your web browser to delete or refuse cookies, please visit the help pages of your web browser. Please note, however, that if you delete cookies or refuse to accept them, you might not be able to experience all of the features we offer, you may not be able to store your preferences, and some of our pages might not display properly.

The Cookies We Use

We classify cookies in the following categories: Strictly Necessary Cookies, Analytics Cookies, Functional Cookies, and Advertising Cookies.

  1. Strictly Necessary Cookies

    Strictly necessary cookies are required for the operation of our website. These cookies enable you to navigate through the website and use essential features, such as logging into secure areas of our site.

    List of strictly necessary cookies

    • Crownpeak
    • Evidon
    • Marketo

  2. Analytics Cookies

    We use analytics cookies to track information about how the website is used so that we can make improvements (for example, to recognize and count the number of visitors and to see how visitors move around our website when they are using it). We also use analytics cookies to test new advertisements, pages, features, or new functionality on the website to see how our users react to them. The information these cookies collect is aggregated, unable to identify you, and therefore, anonymous.

    List of analytics cookies

    • Google Analytics
    • Bizible

  3. Functional Cookies

    Functional cookies are used to recognize you when you return to our website. This enables us to personalize our content for you, greet you by name and remember your preferences. These cookies may be set by us or third-party providers.

    List of functional cookies

    • Demandbase
    • Google Inc.
    • Drift

  4. Advertising Cookies

    Advertising cookies are linked to the website by our trusted partners. They may be used by advertising agencies and our partners to create a profile of your interests and present targeted advertisements on other sites. If you opt out of these cookies, we cannot present advertisements in which you might be interested across different websites

    List of advertising cookies

    • Facebook Business (formerly Facebook Custom Audience)
    • Facebook for Developers (formerly Facebook Connect)
    • Microsoft Advertising
    • AdRoll Group
