    Farewell Carlos Pacheco, illustrator and superhero

    Remembering the life and work of Spanish comic artist, teacher, and legend of the Marvel and DC comics. Domestika is deeply saddened by the passing of Carlos Pacheco, Spanish illustrator, teacher, true superhero of the comic genre, and member of this creative community. In September he announced his ALS (Amyotrophic lateral sclerosis) diagnosis on social media and has died from the degenerative disease aged 60 at the Lnea de la Concepcin hospital in Cdiz.

Cookies Policy

Our Cookies Policy is intended to notify you clearly and thoroughly about how and why we use cookies on the website www.domestika.org (the "Platform"). If you would like to know more about our use of cookies, you may send an email to info@domestika.org.

Cookies

A cookie is a file that is downloaded to your terminal/device (PC or mobile) with the purpose of storing data that may be updated and recovered by the entity that installs them. Our Platform uses "cookies" to collect information about its operation and its users' experience.

We do not store sensible personally identifiable information such as your address, passwords, or credit/debit card information in the cookies we use.

Purpose

Cookies can track information about your visits to our Platform, their frequency and length, the pages viewed and the time spent in our Platform. We may use cookies to:

  • Identify problems to improve our Platform

  • Enable you to better navigate our Platform

  • Offer you a better experience in our Platform

  • Set your apart from other users

We also ask for your consent to use cookies to allow us to obtain more information about your preferences and customize our Platform to meet your interests.

Types of Cookies

Our Platform uses its own cookies and third-party cookies for various purposes, specifically:

Our own cookies

They are sent to your device and managed exclusively by us in order to improve the performance of our Platform. These cookies remain in your browser, allowing us to identify you as a returning user, controlling the user login session, remembering cookies configuration, recognize you as a user in case of managing identification, and customize your content to offer you information according to your preferences (individualization cookies).

Third-party cookies

Third-party cookies are cookies established by a domain that is not our Platform. In our Platform we use two types of third-party cookies:

  • Technical Cookies: They allow you to navigate the Platform and use its services such as controlling data communication, make a purchase, store content, personalize language and currency, format check, share content through social media according to user preferences, speed-up page loading, etc., such as Custora, Olark, Facebook, CloudFare, Visual Website Optimizer.

  • Analytic Cookies: We use Google Analytics, a web analysis tool provided by Google, which allows us to learn how users interact in our Platform. It also enables cookies to know from which website the user came and uses that information to collect data anonymously and create Platform trend reports without identifying individual users. You can find more information regarding the cookies used by Google Analytics and how they work by visiting: developers.google.com/analytics/devguides/collection/analyticsjs/cookie-usage

Consent

When you visit and remain in our Platform you are consenting to the use of cookies as previously explained, under the terms of this Cookies Policy.

Since cookies are not required for using our Platform, you may block or disable them through your browser at any time. If you decide to do so, you will still be able to continue to use our Platform, although some of our services might be limited, and your experience might be less rewarding.

If you wish to revoke your informed consent regarding our cookies policy, you will need to delete them from your device through your browser settings. These are the links for some of the browsers offering information about cookies:
