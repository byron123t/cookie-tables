Pingit for Club cookies policy



We use cookies and similar technologies as part of providing the Pingit for Clubs service to you.
These technologies enable core functionality such as security, network management, and accessibility. The cookies we use are necessary to ensure your security and privacy while using the Pingit for Clubs service. 
























What are cookies and how does Pingit for Clubs use them?

Cookies are technologies we deploy to provide an online service more suited to the device you connect from. When you visit our website from any device (mobile, tablet or PC), we collect information about the device or browser you use to access the site (including device type, operating system, screen resolution and more) and the IP address your device connects from. You can't initiate or complete some activities within our secure online services without these cookies or similar technologies. 
Strictly necessary cookies
These cookies and similar technologies are essential to provide our banking service to you. They are always turned on, as they enable you to move around app and use its features, as well enabling us to prevent and detect fraud, keeping you secure. The data collected is necessary for Pingit for Clubs to operate, to verify your identity and to maintain your security and privacy while using the app. Without this data, the Pingit for Clubs services you have asked for can't be provided. 
Cookies that Pingit doesn’t use 
The Pingit for Clubs app only uses strictly necessary cookies as set out above. Pingit for Clubs doesn't use cookies or similar technologies to collect information about you that is used for marketing purposes or for the purposes covered by the other three categories below. These cookies aren't used

Performance cookies – these cookies collect information about how a website or app is used. For example, which pages are visited most often and whether any error messages are produced from certain pages
Functionality cookies – these cookies allow a website to remember choices a user makes (such as user name, language or region) and tailor the website to provide enhanced features and content
Targeting cookies – these cookies tailor marketing and adverts to the user’s interests
