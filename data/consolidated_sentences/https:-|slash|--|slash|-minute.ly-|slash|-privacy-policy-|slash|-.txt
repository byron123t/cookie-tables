What are Cookies?
A cookie is a small piece of text that is sent to a user’s browser or device
The browser provides this piece of text to the device of the originating user when this user returns.
A “session cookie” is temporary and will remain on your device until you leave the Site.
A “persistent” cookie may be used to help save your settings and customizations across visits
It will remain on your device until you delete it.
First-party cookies are placed by us, while third-party cookies may be placed by a third party
We use both first- and third-party cookies.
We may use the terms “cookies” to refer to all technologies that we may use to store data in your browser or device or that collect information or help us identify you in the manner described above, such as web beacons or “pixel tags”.
