Generally, cookies enable our clients to target ads to users, to evaluate how well their ads work, and to see users’ opt-out choices
For detailed information on our cookies usage purposes and for general commitment towards our platform and services privacy, see at https://site.adform.com/privacy-center/platform-privacy/product-and-services-privacy-policy/.
