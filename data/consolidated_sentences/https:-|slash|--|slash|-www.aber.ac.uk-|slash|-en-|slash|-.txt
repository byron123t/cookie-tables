Discover Aberystwyth
Aberystwyth University is located on the coast of west Wales, nestled between the Cambrian Mountains and Cardigan Bay
Aberystwyth is a diverse and welcoming community in a historic seaside market town that has a cosmopolitan Welsh culture
with so much on offer, you’ll be spoilt for choice.
