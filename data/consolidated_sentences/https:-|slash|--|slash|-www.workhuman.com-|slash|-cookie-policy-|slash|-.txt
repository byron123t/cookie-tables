What are cookies?
Cookies are small text files that are set by us or the third-party providers listed below and stored by your browser on your computer’s hard drive or mobile device
Cookies cannot introduce viruses into your computer, and we will never use cookies to search your computer or mobile device.
In addition to cookies, Workhuman uses similar technologies like web beacons and transparent GIF files that can recognize certain types of information on your computer
These technologies are typically used in connection with cookies and may be used to help us analyze Site visits or help us track whether emails have been viewed or clicked.
